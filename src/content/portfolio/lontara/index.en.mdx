## Context

Lontara is a regional tax and levy payment application developed as part of a local
government service digitalisation initiative. This project ran alongside Patonro, extending
Banua Coder's experience in the same domain but with a different client and scope.

Having two similar projects in the portfolio is not repetition — each implementation brought
new lessons about variations in regional administrative flows, differences in tax data
structures across districts, and UX nuances for different user profiles.

## What We Built

Lontara was built with Flutter and Laravel, with an emphasis on ease of use:

- **Taxpayer authentication**: Login by NIK or NPWP so users do not need to create a new
  account.
- **Bills by category**: Land and building tax, vehicle levies, and other categories
  displayed in easily navigable tabs.
- **Integrated payment**: One-click payment flow after a bill is selected, with immediate
  confirmation and a saveable receipt.
- **History and filters**: Users can view all transactions and filter by year or category.

## Impact

Lontara is deployed and actively used by residents. Together with Patonro, both projects
position Banua Coder as an experienced team in the regional digital tax domain — a
specialisation that is increasingly relevant as local government digitisation agendas
expand across Indonesia.
