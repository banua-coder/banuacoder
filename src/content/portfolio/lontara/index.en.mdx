## Context

Lontara is the province-scale regional tax and levy payment application for South Sulawesi,
developed in partnership with PT Brantas Inti Utama. Its scope covers every regency and city
in South Sulawesi that participates in the PT Brantas Inti Utama digital regional tax program.

Together with Patonro (regency scale, Gowa), Lontara rounds out Banua Coder's understanding
of variations in regional administrative flows, differences in tax data structures across
districts, and UX nuances for a wide and diverse user base.

## What We Built

Lontara was built with Flutter and Laravel, with an emphasis on ease of use:

- **Taxpayer authentication**: Login by NIK or NPWP so users do not need to create a new
  account.
- **Bills by category**: Land and building tax, vehicle levies, and other categories
  displayed in easily navigable tabs.
- **Integrated payment**: One-click payment flow after a bill is selected, with immediate
  confirmation and a saveable receipt.
- **History and filters**: Users can view all transactions and filter by year or category.

## Impact

Lontara is deployed and actively used by taxpayers across the partner regencies and cities
in South Sulawesi. Together with Patonro, both projects — alongside PT Brantas Inti Utama
as the implementation partner — position Banua Coder as an experienced team in the regional
digital tax domain across both regency and provincial scales.
