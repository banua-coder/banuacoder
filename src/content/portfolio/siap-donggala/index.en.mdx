## Context

Siap Donggala is a personnel management information system built for Pemerintah Kabupaten
Donggala, Central Sulawesi. The project addresses the need for HR governance digitalization
within a local government environment — a challenge that is not only technical but also
requires deep understanding of civil service regulations and the diverse operational needs
of different work units.

Developing systems for regional government agencies demands careful consideration: the data
handled is sensitive, business processes are often complex, and end users span a wide range
of digital literacy levels.

## What We Built

Siap Donggala was built as a comprehensive personnel management platform:

- **Employee master data**: Full employee profile management including personal data,
  positions, rank/grade, and career history in an integrated system.
- **Digital attendance**: Accurate and auditable attendance recording, replacing manual
  systems vulnerable to manipulation.
- **Leave and permit management**: Structured leave request and approval flows with
  automatic notifications to relevant supervisors.
- **Personnel reporting**: Dashboards and reports that help leadership monitor personnel
  conditions in real time.

## Impact

Siap Donggala is live and actively used by the personnel unit of Pemerintah Kabupaten
Donggala. This project strengthens Banua Coder's track record in building information
systems for regional government — a segment that demands high trust, regulatory awareness,
and the ability to handle sensitive data to the right standards.
