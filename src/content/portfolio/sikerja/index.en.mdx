## Context

Sikerja is a mobile employment portal app for the people of Central Sulawesi, built
as a partnership between the Central Sulawesi Provincial Government and corporate
partners across the province. Its goal: give Sulteng job seekers a single trusted
channel to find official listings supplied by partner companies, and give partner
companies an official channel to reach local talent with the provincial government
as the verification layer.

## What We Built

- **Job-seeker mobile app**: Search-first interface with filters for location,
  industry, and experience level; job-seeker profile; and a lightweight application flow.
- **Partner-company dashboard**: Admin side for partner companies to publish listings,
  manage incoming applications, and view basic analytics.
- **Pemprov verification system**: Backend that lets the provincial team verify partner
  companies and monitor the quality of published listings.
- **Push notifications**: Job seekers receive alerts for new listings matching their
  profile and filters.

## Impact

Sikerja is one of the Central Sulawesi provincial government's digital employment
initiatives that was built with engineering depth — not just a job board listing.
The official partnership with the provincial government and partner corporations
gives the platform a different position from generic job boards: listings on Sikerja
have already passed the provincial verification layer, so Sulteng job seekers can
use it with a higher level of trust.
