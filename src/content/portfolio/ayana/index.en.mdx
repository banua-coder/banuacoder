## Context

Ayana is a pregnancy and child-development companion app built for Indonesian
parents. Its vision — *"Every Heartbeat, Every Step of Growth — Always close
with Ayana"* (translated from "Setiap Detak, Setiap Tumbuh – Selalu dekat
dengan Ayana") — captures what makes the product different from typical
health apps: not just a tracking tool, but a companion present at every phase,
from week one of pregnancy through child growth milestones.

What makes this domain challenging: a good parenting/health app must balance
credible medical grounding with a tone that stays warm and approachable. Ayana
roots its tracking in the standards of the Indonesian Ministry of Health
(Kemenkes RI) and the Indonesian Pediatric Society (IDAI) — but presents them
in language and visuals that feel like a conversation with a friend, not a
clinical report.

## What We Built

Banua Coder developed Ayana as a modern web application combining Laravel 12
as the backend, Vue 3 as the frontend, Inertia.js as the SPA-like bridge,
TypeScript for type safety, and Tailwind CSS v4 for a consistent design system.
Core modules include:

- **Week-by-week pregnancy tracking**: Fetal development updates, common
  symptoms, and recommended actions, mapped to gestational age.
- **Maternal nutrition**: Trimester-appropriate nutrition guidance with food
  recommendations relevant to the Indonesian context.
- **Child growth milestones**: Tracking of height, weight, and developmental
  milestones with Kemenkes RI reference curves.
- **Child health content**: A health-information library validated against
  IDAI guidelines, with parent-friendly navigation.
- **Routine reminders**: Notifications for prenatal checks, child vaccinations,
  and growth-monitoring schedules.
- **Warm brand identity**: Soft pink/coral palette, Inter typography, and
  micro-interactions that keep the app's tone supportive rather than clinical.

## Impact

Ayana fills a real gap in the Indonesian market: a pregnancy and parenting
application in Indonesian, grounded in credible medical standards, but delivered
in a tone that doesn't intimidate young parents.

For Banua Coder, this project adds depth to our consumer healthtech portfolio
with genuinely complex medical-content domain — alongside Detexi and
Caretaker.id — and stands as one of our strongest references for combining
domain depth with warm product craft.
