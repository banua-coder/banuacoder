## Context

Detexi Doctor is a digital healthcare application providing easy access to doctor consultations
and medical information directly from a smartphone. As demand for quick and affordable
healthcare grows, Detexi bridges the gap between users and professional medical staff
without geographic limitations.

Healthcare has a higher quality bar than most consumer applications — user trust, information
accuracy, and data privacy are non-negotiable. Banua Coder's involvement in this project
reflects the client's confidence in the engineering and UX standards the team applies.

## What We Built

The Detexi Doctor mobile application was built with Flutter, with a focus on reliability
and ease of use:

- **Doctor search and selection**: Filter by specialty, availability, and ratings to help
  users find the right medical professional.
- **Consultation flow**: Straightforward appointment booking with automatic confirmation
  and schedule reminders.
- **Medical communication**: A text-based consultation interface enabling secure, structured
  exchange of health information.
- **Health history**: Personal consultation records and medical document management
  accessible at any time.

## Impact

Detexi Doctor was successfully published as a live product accessible to the public.
This project strengthens Banua Coder's capability in the healthcare domain — one of the
most demanding consumer application segments in terms of trust, privacy, and UX quality.
